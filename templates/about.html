    About

    Unbeatable Tic-Tac-Toe is my second large project and was inspired by this Wikipedia article on how a person might play a perfect game of tic-tac-toe. As someone who is interested in the design of modern chess playing computers, I thought that starting with tic-tac-toe would be a good way to get an introduction to how a programmer might go about developing software that can play games. Ultimately, I would love to build a chess engine using more advanced algorithms such as neural networks.

    Throughout this project, I've had the opportunity to gain experience in Python, JavaScript, and PostgreSQL.
